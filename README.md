# 🍊 Swiggy Sales Analytics Dashboard — Microsoft Excel

An end-to-end interactive sales analytics dashboard built in **Microsoft Excel**, analyzing real-world food delivery data from Swiggy. Designed to deliver executive-level business intelligence through dynamic KPI reporting, multi-dimensional data visualizations, and self-service data exploration.

## ✅ Features

- **5 KPI Summary Cards** — Total Sales, Average Rating, Average Order Value, Rating Count, Total Orders for instant executive snapshot
- **Monthly Sales Trend** — Line chart tracking sales from Jan–Aug to identify seasonal patterns
- **Daily Sales Trend** — Bar chart across Mon–Sun revealing peak order days
- **Weekly Sales Trend** — 36-week granular bar chart for long-term trend analysis
- **Sales by Food Type** — Donut chart splitting Non-Veg (91%) vs Veg (9%) revenue contribution
- **Top 5 Cities by Sales** — Horizontal ranked bar chart: Bengaluru, Lucknow, Hyderabad, Mumbai, New Delhi
- **Sales by State Map** — Geographic visualization of state-wise revenue distribution
- **Quarterly Performance Table** — Q1–Q3 summary with conditional formatting for Sales, Rating, and Orders
- **Multi-Field Slicers** — Filter by Category, Month, and Restaurant Name for self-service data exploration

## 💡 Key Insights from the Data

- **Bengaluru** leads all cities with ₹5.46M in sales — nearly 75% more than the second-ranked city
- **Non-Veg** food dominates with 91% of total revenue vs 9% for Veg
- **Saturday** is the highest-grossing day (₹7.78M), while **Tuesday** is the lowest (₹7.36M)
- **Q1 and Q2** perform almost equally (~₹19.67M and ₹19.90M), while Q3 trails at ₹13.44M
- Average customer rating is consistently **4.3** across all quarters — indicating stable service quality

## 🔧 How to Use

1. **Download** `Swiggy_Dashboard.xlsx` from this repository
2. **Open** in Microsoft Excel 2016 or later (recommended: Excel 365)
3. Use the **Slicers** on the left panel to filter by Category, Month, or Restaurant Name
4. All charts and KPI cards **update dynamically** based on your selection
5. Reset filters by clicking **Clear Filter** on any slicer
